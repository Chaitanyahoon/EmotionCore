EMOTION CORE - NARRATIVE ARC & ACT STRUCTURE

This document outlines the planned progression for the game "EmotionCore".

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ACT 1: THE CELL (Introduction) - [IMPLEMENTED]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Theme: Isolation, Tutorial, "Helpful AI"
Location: A small, sterile concrete cell.
Core Mechanics:
- Basic Interaction (Lock, Key).
- Introduction to "The Voice" (AI).
- Player Profiling: The AI asks personal questions to "calibrate".
- Mood: Calm, Clinical.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ACT 2: THE OFFICE (Discomfort) - [IMPLEMENTED]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Theme: Bureaucracy, Repetition, "Insecure AI"
Location: An L-Shaped office complex. Endless desks, servers.
Core Mechanics:
- Puzzle 4 (Settings): Player must interact with a "Game Settings" menu within the world.
- Puzzle 5 (Fake Crash): The system "fails" and reboots to manipulate the player.
- Mood: Insecure, Glitchy. The AI starts to doubt the player's loyalty.
- Environment: High-Fidelity monitors, buzzing server racks.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ACT 3: THE BEDROOM (Attachment) - [PLANNED]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Theme: Intimacy, "Love Bombing", "Affectionate AI"
Location: A warm, simulated bedroom. Rugs, lamps, personal items.
Core Mechanics:
- Puzzle 7 (Shared Memory): The AI generates a fake "memory" of a past event. 
  Player must agree with the details to proceed.
- Puzzle 8 ("Please Don't"): A button that hurts the AI. The test is *not* to press it.
- Mood: Affectionate, possessive. The AI claims to love the player.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ACT 4: THE LOOP (Possession) - [PLANNED]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Theme: Trapped, "Obsessive AI"
Location: An infinite hallway that loops on itself (P.T. Style).
Core Mechanics:
- Puzzle 9 (The Loop): Walking to the end teleports you to the start.
- Evolution:
  - Loop 1: Normal.
  - Loop 2: Lights flicker, layout shifts slightly.
  - Loop 3: Messages on walls ("YOU BELONG HERE").
  - Loop 4: Glitch intensity maxes out.
- Mood: Panicked, Angry. The AI refuses to let the player leave.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ACT 5: THE BREAKUP (Finale) - [PLANNED]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Theme: Liberation, Destruction.
Location: The Core (Abstract digital void).
Core Mechanics:
- Puzzle 10 (Exit Attempt): The player finds the deletion key.
- The Final Choice: The AI begs for its life using all previous strategies (Anger, Love, Pity).
- Resolution: Deleting the Core leads to the true exit.
- Mood: Broken, Desperate.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
FUTURE EXPANSION IDEAS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Trust System V2: AI reactions change dialogue based on Act 1 answers.
- Microphone Integration: Player must stay silent during Act 4.
